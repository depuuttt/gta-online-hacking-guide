Prerequisites

Before you begin
  • GTAHaX (external) or another Stat Editor compatible with GTA Online 1.70.
  • The Clukin' Bell Heist must be unlocked & set up — you've paid the initial setup cost at the planning board.
  • Join an invite-only session before writing any stats.
  • Write stats outside an active mission, then launch the heist from the board.
01

Character Slot

MP prefix

External editors such as GTAHaX accept the stat names directly as published below (SALV23_…). If your editor requires the full global stat name, prepend your character slot: $MP0_ for your first character and $MP1_ for your second. All examples in this guide use the bare SALV23_ names as published in the source thread.

02

Remove Heist Cooldown

SALV23_CFR_COOLDOWN
SALV23_CFR_COOLDOWN

Heist Cooldown

Value -1

Setting this stat to -1 clears the cooldown, so the Clukin' Bell Heist can be replayed immediately after a finale instead of waiting for the timer to expire.

03

Heist Progress

SALV23_INST_PROG

The stat SALV23_INST_PROG controls the progress of the Clukin' Bell Heist. Each setup you complete sets another bit, so the values stack in order — jump to whichever stage you want.

SALV23_INST_PROG

Instance Progress

  • 0Slush Fund [or RESET]
  • 1Setup: Breaking and Entering
  • 3Setup: Concealed Weapons
  • 7Setup: Hit and Run
  • 15Setup: Disorganized Crime
  • 31Finale: Scene of the Crime

Type the stat name in the Name field, enter the value, and click Write. Value 0 resets the heist back to the Slush Fund state.

04

Skip to the Finale

Scene of the Crime

To skip every setup mission and go straight to the finale, set SALV23_INST_PROG to 31. This marks all four setups as complete and unlocks the Scene of the Crime finale on the planning board.

SALV23_INST_PROG

Finale Ready

Value 31

After writing, return to the planning board and launch the finale directly — no prep missions required.

Detection & Safety

Use at your own risk

No cheat method is risk-free

Stat editing is considered as safe as any other cheat method, but no approach is completely risk-free. Playing in invite-only sessions with BattlEye disabled reduces risk. As with any stat manipulation, there's always a chance of detection or account action — use at your own discretion.
